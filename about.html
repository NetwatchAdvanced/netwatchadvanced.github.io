What I've actually built

The same system running on my own network.

Everything NetWatch deploys for clients runs on my own hardware at home first. The IDS, the DNS stack, the dashboard, the firewall rules — I live with the data it produces daily. That's not a sales point, it's just how the development worked. Real network, real traffic, real anomalies.

I hold a B.S. in Cybersecurity with a concentration in Data Analytics, conferred in 2026, alongside several Google professional certifications in cybersecurity, data analytics, and IT support. The academic foundation matters, but the honest reason I can do this work is that I've been doing it — iterating on a live system, troubleshooting real problems, and building tools that have to actually work.

What to expect

Small by design. Intentionally.

In-person only

Every deployment involves an on-site visit. Remote-only setup isn't something I offer — the network has to be seen to be understood.

Limited client capacity

A large roster means diluted attention. The number of active clients I take on at once is deliberate — every network gets a real eye on it.

No jargon

You don't need to know what a SPAN port is to benefit from one. I explain what's relevant in plain language and skip what isn't.

Honest about limitations

NetWatch is a detection system. It doesn't prevent threats. I'll tell you exactly what it does and doesn't do before any commitment is made.

Ready to have a conversation? The free consultation is 20–30 minutes — no commitment, no pitch. Just an honest look at your network and whether any of this makes sense for you.
