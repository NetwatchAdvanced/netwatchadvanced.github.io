Let's talk about
your network.

The free consultation is a 20–30 minute call. No sales pitch — just an honest conversation about your current setup, what you're trying to address, and whether NetWatch is the right fit.

Send us a message

netwatch@protonmail.com

We respond within 24 hours. If you don't hear back, check your spam folder — Proton-to-Proton mail is fine but Proton-to-Gmail sometimes misfires.

Or book a time directly

Online scheduling coming soon — drop us an email for now and we'll find a time that works.

What to include in your message

  • A rough description of your current setup — router brand, number of devices, ISP if you know it
  • What you're trying to address — coverage issues, security concerns, or just curious where to start
  • Your general location — NetWatch is in-person only, so this confirms we can actually serve you
  • Any urgency — if something feels actively wrong, say so

What happens next

01

You reach out

Send a brief email with your setup and what you're looking to address. No technical expertise required — plain English is fine.

02

We respond within 24 hours

We'll confirm we can serve your area and schedule a free 20–30 minute call to talk through your situation.

03

Free consultation call

We discuss your network, your concerns, and which tier of service actually makes sense for you. No commitment required.

04

Written recommendation

After the call you receive a written summary of what we'd recommend and why — yours to keep regardless of whether you move forward.

In-person deployments only. NetWatch installs require an on-site visit — we don't do remote-only setup. We currently serve the [your metro area] area. If you're outside that range, reach out anyway — coverage may expand.
