What to include in your message

  • Type of business and rough size: bar, restaurant, office, retail, etc. The context helps us understand what the network needs to support.
  • Your current setup: router brand if you know it, ISP, number of locations, whether you have separate networks for staff and guests.
  • What you're trying to address: coverage issues, security concerns, compliance questions, or just want a professional assessment of where you stand.
  • Your general location: NetWatch Advanced is in-person only, so this confirms we can serve you.
  • Any urgency: if something feels actively wrong right now, say so.
