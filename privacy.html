Privacy Policy

What this site does and doesn't collect.

NetWatch is a privacy and security service. This site is held to the same standard. Plain language, no ambiguity.

Last updated: 2026 — This policy applies to the NetWatch website only. For the data practices of the NetWatch monitoring application deployed on client networks, see the service agreement provided at time of deployment.

This website

The NetWatch website (this site) does not use analytics tracking, advertising scripts, or third-party cookies of any kind. No tracking pixels, no session recording, no behavioral profiling. What you read here stays between you and your browser.

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) as part of normal hosting infrastructure. This is outside NetWatch's control — GitHub's privacy policy governs that data. NetWatch does not receive, store, or process those logs.

Contact and consultation requests

If you contact NetWatch by email, the information you provide — your name, email address, and any details about your network or setup — is used solely to respond to your inquiry and, if you proceed, to scope and deliver services. It is not shared with third parties, used for marketing, or retained beyond the active client relationship.

Email is currently handled through Proton Mail. Proton's end-to-end encryption applies to Proton-to-Proton communication. For other providers, standard email security applies.

NetWatch application — client deployments

The NetWatch monitoring application, when deployed on a client network, collects the following data locally on the client's hardware:

  • Network traffic metadata — source and destination IPs, ports, protocols, timestamps
  • Suricata IDS alert events and signatures matched
  • DNS query logs — domains queried, query types, allow/block status, per-device history
  • Device inventory — IP addresses, MAC addresses, assigned device names, first/last seen
  • Geographic connection data — resolved locally via a local database, no external API calls

All data remains on the client's hardware. Nothing is transmitted to NetWatch servers or any third party. The operator accesses client dashboards remotely via Tailscale for support and monitoring purposes — this access is scoped to the NetWatch application and does not involve copying or retaining client network data offsite.

On service termination, the client retains full ownership of all data on their hardware. NetWatch retains no copy of client network data after the engagement ends.

Cookies

This website does not set cookies. No consent banner is required or displayed because there is nothing to consent to.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date. Active clients will be notified directly.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to netwatch@protonmail.com .
